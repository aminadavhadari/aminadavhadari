ECO - Engineering Change Order

ECO module designed to trace the activities performed in the BOM and to manage the implementation of the changes at a future date.
The module in the ERP system - Priority is relatively limited and it mainly deals with the logistics side.

    The various projects for the implementation of the ECO module include:
  1. Analysis of requirements and needs.
  2. Implementation the module in all the related departments in the company.
  3. Writing work instruction.
  4. Writing work instruction.
  5. Development of the module to support the following features:

Manage / tracking of Alternative Parts.
Bulk replacement of several items in several fathers.
Manage a new Parts.
Management of Documents.
Management of Procedures.
Management of Part Pricing.
Expanding the module to document the impacts and decisions that accompany ECO approval (include: ECR – Engineering Change request ).
Link / Interface with an Engineering system.
Interaction of work in two companies (engineering company, manufacturing company).
Comparison reports Current to Previous ECO.
